About Us

At Donovan Metal, we don't just see junk—we see raw potential. For years, we've been the go-to crew for clearing out the heavy, the broken, and the rusted. Whether it's a busted refrigerator, a heap of auto parts, or an old washer taking up space, we haul it away for completely free.

We are committed to keeping our community clean, recycling responsibly, and doing the heavy lifting so you don't have to. Fast, reliable, and tough enough to handle any job—that's the Donovan way.
