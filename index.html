About Us

Forged in the heart of Ontario, Donovans Heavy Metal Recycling isn't just a scrapyard; we are the end of the line for your unwanted iron, steel, and appliances. We believe that heavy metal shouldn't rust away in your driveway or crowd your garage. We offer lightning-fast, free pickups to clear your space, recycling materials back into the grid with industrial efficiency. No job is too rusty, no haul is too heavy. You stack it, we scrap it.
